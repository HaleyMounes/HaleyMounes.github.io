PhotoGrammetry

Photogrammetry is the technique of producing 3D models from photos taken of an object. These were done with the program Meshroom, of fellow artist’s sculptures Amanda Jenkins and Henry Watts. As an attempt at digitizing an art medium that normally does not translate well to flat images, they try to create scenes that add to the value of the sculpture.

Synesthesia

The group exhibition Synesthesia is an exploration of Arduino and interactive art. This interactive exhibit invites the viewer to stand before a transparent screen displaying several photogrammatized floral arrangements. The flowers track the position of the viewer in the room, via carbon paint strips on the floor. In the web version, you may move the model via the keyboard.
