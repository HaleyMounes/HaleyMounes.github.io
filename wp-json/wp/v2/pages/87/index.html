Haley Mounes is a multi-media artists working out of Bellingham, Washington, witha\u00a0 focus in the blending of interactive technology and painting. Earning her BFA in 2021 at Western Washington University, she graduated with honors as the Outstanding Artist<\/em> of the year. Her views on contemporary art are influenced by international travel to the Virginia Center for the Creative Arts collaboration Figure and Symbol<\/em> Residency in Auvillar, France, as well as the Metro Art Access,<\/em> New York trip. Awarded the fall Research and Creative Activities Grant for her exhibition Material Mind, <\/em>and Bennerstrom Award for painting two years in a row, she has continued to emerge in her showings both online at Outside.In<\/em><\/a>, and publicly in\u00a0 Song For Five Zones<\/em>.<\/a><\/p>
